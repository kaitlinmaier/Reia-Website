Mission

Reia is a team of three female engineers and a urogynecologist working to redesign non-surgical pelvic organ prolapse (POP) treatment options. Collectively, they are dissatisfied with the current treatment options for POP and are passionate about improving the lives of women affected by it.

Reia is conducting interviews with women who have sought treatment for their POP and are willing to share their stories and options throughout Reia's design process.
